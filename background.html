Background

Understanding Australia’s Speeding Enforcement.

This project distils the Bureau of Infrastructure and Transport Research Economics (BITRE) enforcement dataset (2008–2024) into clear, interactive visuals. Instead of overwhelming tables and static reports, our dashboard highlights meaningful trends in speeding fines, detection methods, jurisdictions, locations, and demographic patterns.

Support Data-Driven Decisions

Policymakers can identify high-risk segments and allocate enforcement resources more effectively using evidence from the dashboard.

Make Road Safety Insights Accessible

Interactive visuals lower technical barriers, enabling researchers and the public to explore enforcement data with ease.

Understand Driver Behaviour

Age groups, monthly seasonality and detection methods help reveal how driving patterns change over time.

Reveal Enforcement Patterns

Discover which jurisdictions and location types show sustained or rising enforcement activity.
