Visualisation Section

Our dashboard transforms BITRE's speeding–enforcement dataset into clear, interactive visuals that help you explore how Australia's road-safety landscape has evolved from 2008 to 2024.

  • Track how speeding fines change over time and compare detection methods.
  • See which jurisdictions lead, which ones improve, and where enforcement has intensified.
  • Understand how the balance between police-issued and camera-issued fines shifts across regions and years.
  • Identify which age groups contribute the most fines and how demographic patterns have changed.
  • Discover monthly peaks and how patterns differ by detection method.
  • Explore where fines are concentrated—urban, regional, remote—and how these areas change over time.
